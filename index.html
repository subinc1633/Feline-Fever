about

Feline Fever is a rhythm game that involves pressing the respective arrows on the screen at the right time.
To obtain points, the player must match the scrolling arrows with the stationary arrows at the bottom of the screen.

how to play

Use the arrow keys or WASD to press the game buttons.
You may mute the music if you wish by clicking the music note on the top left of the screen.
